Developing Digital Skills

Digital skills are essential for global competitiveness, providing access to opportunities to individuals and communities. Let some of our stories inspire you to action.

Action

Volunteer

Level of complexity

Low

Time Required

2-3 hours

Cost

None

Why it matters

Digital skills are essential for global competitiveness, providing job opportunities, and growth for communities. Women are 20% less likely to be online and the percentage rises in lower-income countries.

Building digital skills at an early age, providing technical skills training, and engaging with role models is central to driving digital equality and ensuring more diverse people have the skills necessary to participate and prosper in our digital world.

What you can do

  • Team up with your local community to deliver digital training or career sharing session to young generation.
  • Leverage the platform and content of Women Rock-IT to provide technical skills training in your local community.
  • Share your story to increase the impact.
  • Additional Ideas
    • Recruit volunteers to share their career journey and how they got into STEM
    • Host a girls’ night out watch party or host an event at your children’s school or community center

You can host a Women Rock-IT event at your workplace or a local venue, using online resources to inspire and engage students.

Action

Level of complexity

Time Required

Why it matters

The increased shift towards digitalization is creating a divide and resulting in some communities being left behind.

Converting disused community centers, or a school facility as an example, into a digital space is an effective way to ensure young people acquire digital literacy skills and access digital learning.

What you can do

Team up with your chosen charitable organization to increase connectivity and create opportunity in your local community or abroad.

Using Technology for Good

Use your networking and collaboration expertise to empower local charities and transform their community support.

Level of complexity

Why it matters

Charitable organizations often struggle with understanding what is needed and how to deploy, fund, and acquire the basic networking and collaboration infrastructure required to support their efforts to address the needs of their beneficiaries.

Reliable and suitable equipment can sometimes be the difference between life and death.

What you can do

Lend your industry skills and experience to make an impact for a cause important to you.
